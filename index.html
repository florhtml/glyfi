Between 3400 and 3200 BCE, people communicated with hieroglyphs.
Well, in 2020, they continue to do so. But now they call it emojis. 😅
So, hieroglyphs + emojis = Welcome to Glyfi!
Glyfi is a fan and fast emoji chat system with voice recognition
to communicate with friends.
Forget typing. Forget to send or listen to audio messages.
Glyfi uses NLP with a speech-to-text module from the Natural Language Toolkit platform (but in this case, we could call it a speech-to-📧⛰🌊🐗🇮🇳 module).
Easy! Faster! Funny!
Yeah! A glyf is much better than thousand words.
